How to View a Calc

Use your downloaded calc data with the public Dynamic Calc Decomps viewer.

  1. Navigate to https://hzla.github.io/Dynamic-Calc-Decomps/?dev=1
  2. Upload the downloaded calc data via the Upload Data button on the top nav bar.
  3. Set up calc settings from the nav bar. Recommended calc settings for most Gen 3 ROM hacks are shown below.

How to View a Dex

Use your ROM and layout files with the public ddex viewer.

  1. Visit https://ddex-chi.vercel.app/
  2. Click Load Gen3/4 ROM.
  3. Upload both the .gba and .toml files.
